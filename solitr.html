Solitr

Play the classic Solitaire card game with a modern twist

How to Play

  • • Build foundation piles up from Ace to King by suit
  • • Create tableau piles in descending order with alternating colors
  • • Move cards between piles to uncover face-down cards
  • • Use the stock pile when you run out of moves
  • • Win by moving all cards to the foundation piles
