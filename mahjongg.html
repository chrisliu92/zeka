Mahjongg Solitaire

Match tiles and test your strategy in this classic Chinese puzzle game

How to Play

  • • Match identical tiles to remove them from the board
  • • Only tiles that are "free" (not blocked) can be matched
  • • Plan your moves carefully to clear all tiles
  • • Use hints if you get stuck
