Gameplay Guide: How to Play Escape Road

Prepare for an Intense Escape Adventure

Escape Road offers an exciting and challenging gameplay experience that keeps you on the edge of your seat. In Escape Road, you take on the role of a skilled driver, maneuvering your car through a sprawling city while evading relentless police pursuit. As soon as you make your escape from the bank, police cars will chase you with blaring sirens, ready to catch you at the slightest mistake. One wrong move, and the game ends instantly.

To succeed in Escape Road, you must expertly navigate crowded streets, avoid tall buildings, lakes, and unexpected obstacles. Stay alert—if you drive into a lake or get trapped by police blocking all exits, your escape will be over. The key to mastering Escape Road is to prolong your chase for as long as possible, collecting rewards and proving your driving skills in this thrilling online game.

Collect Money and Gifts in Escape Road

In Escape Road, every race rewards you with a mystery gift box containing a random cash prize. You can also find money scattered throughout the streets during your escape—be sure to collect these valuable rewards. The cash you gather in Escape Road allows you to purchase powerful supercars, making your getaway even more thrilling and dangerous.

Upgrade Your Car in Escape Road

To unlock the best supercars in Escape Road, accumulate as much money as possible and use the lucky spin feature. Escape Road features 90 unique supercars, each offering special abilities to help you extend your escape. Spend your collected money to spin for a chance at a random supercar and enhance your performance in Escape Road.

Escape Road Controls

A/D: Start your car.

A/D or arrow keys: Steer left or right.

W or up arrow: Accelerate.

S or down arrow: Reverse.
