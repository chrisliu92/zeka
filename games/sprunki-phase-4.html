How to Play: Game Instructions

Tap to the Beat: In Sprunki Phase 4, synchronize your taps with the falling notes on the screen to rack up points and move through the levels.

Timing is Everything: The accuracy of your taps directly affects your score; the more precise you are, the greater your rewards.

Build Chain Combos: Hit multiple notes in succession to create combos that multiply your score, adding excitement to your gameplay.

Avoid the Obstacles: Keep your focus sharp and steer clear of the red obstacles to maintain your streak and maximize your scoring potential.
