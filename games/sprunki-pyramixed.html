How to Play Sprunki Pyramixed: Gameplay Mechanics Overview

Drag-and-Drop System: In Sprunki Pyramixed, players can effortlessly drag musical elements onto characters to activate various sounds, including vocals, beats, melodies, and effects.

Unlocking Bonuses: Explore hidden combos known as Bonuses in Sprunki Pyramixed. By merging specific characters, you can unlock cinematic sequences that enrich your Music Rush experience.

Layered Music Creation: Mix and match sounds to develop dynamic compositions. Each new element you add transforms the music, keeping the gameplay engaging and fresh.
